Learning through the Human Experience

After interviewing Jordan, he shared a few projects he has been putting off for various reasons. He expressed a strong desire to finally see these projects through, as they require much creativity, and he is very excited about them. Jordan has always been a builder by nature. He loves tackling problems and finding solutions, something many of you may relate to.

Jordan is about to take on two very different but equally intriguing projects. As a huge fan of classic rock and playing guitar, he is going to attempt to build his own electric guitar for the first time. Additionally, as a lover of sourdough bread, he will also try making sourdough bread from scratch. This website will showcase the chronological process and the end results of his attempts at both projects.
